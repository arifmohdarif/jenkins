EUROTECH TECHNICAL SOLUTIONS PVT. LTD.

 

UGOLINI offers to textile firms from all over the world its wide array of products, easily divided in four production categories: dyeing machines, laboratory equipment, dyeing stainless equipment, drying machines, centrifuges and automatic dyeing.

 

In these last years, also thanks to the collaboration with the major textile groups in the world, Ugolini has focused more and more in the manufacture of dyeing machines for small productions, carving a respectable place for itself in the global field of textile industries.
