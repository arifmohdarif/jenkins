Who We Are

 

Eurotech technical solutions private Ltd.  is a professionally managed company engaged in sales and services of Quality textile machinery.

 

The Company Was founded in the year 2008 and got incorporated as private limited under Companies Act 1956 in 2009.

 

As a company we are determine to expand steadily, with a clear perspective to provide quality equipment and commendable services to our customers.

 

ETTSPL has been always kept inherent to employee’s mentality of work and strategy of its future, incorporating constant innovation in our service and products and offering the best solutions to all our customers.
